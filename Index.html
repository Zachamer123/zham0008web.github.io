Candelo's Weekly Pick

"Dark Emu" by Bruce Pascoe is a thought-provoking book that challenges the traditional view of pre-colonial Indigenous Australian societies as simple hunter-gatherers. Pascoe presents evidence of advanced agricultural practices and settlement patterns, highlighting the richness and diversity of Indigenous cultures before European colonization. The book serves as a critique of the erasure of Aboriginal history and prompts reflection on the ongoing legacy of colonialism.
